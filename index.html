Utilities & Retail Energy Providers

Customers are no longer just consuming electricity — they are generating solar power, storing energy in batteries, charging EVs, exporting to the grid, shifting load, and creating complex billing disputes that legacy CIS systems cannot easily explain.

EV Fleet Payment Companies

Fleet-payment companies like WEX and Corpay need to reimburse employees for charging company EVs at home. The electricity used may come from the grid, rooftop solar, a home battery, or a mix of all three. The cost also changes by tariff, time of use, location, and driver behavior.

No reliable financial-grade way to know exactly how much reimbursable energy was used, what it actually cost, and whether the claim is accurate.
Result: overpayment risk, fraud risk, driver disputes, poor reimbursement experience.
Across all these customers, the missing layer is the same: a trusted system that can connect meter data, behind-the-meter device activity, tariffs, customer behavior, and enterprise billing systems, and turn them into auditable transactions.
